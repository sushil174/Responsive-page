About Me

Hey there! I’m Gengar, once known as a mischievous Ghost/Poison-type Pokémon lurking in the shadows, but I’ve traded in my spooky moves for something far more exciting—web development! After years of battling in arenas and playing tricks on my opponents with moves like Shadow Ball and Hypnosis, I decided it was time for a change. While sneaking around and causing a little chaos was fun, I started to feel that I wanted to use my skills for something creative, something that built things, rather than destroyed them. That’s when I discovered the world of coding. It felt like magic—a different kind, of course—where I could create my own virtual realms instead of haunting other people's.
